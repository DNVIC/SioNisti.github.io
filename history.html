The Story


Chapter 1 - The beginning


I got the idea of the "Toad%" (later renamed to 6 Toad) category sometime in november of 2017 when i saw DNVIC running Super Mario 64 16 Star.
He was in the HMC split and had to talk to toad for the star but instead puching him many times i laughed at this maybe too much and jokingly said "ill make toad%".
So i fired up Super Mario 64 and started looking for the toad locations and i found 8 of them but 2 of them needed a certain ammount of stars to appear.
The one at HMC needed 12 and the one next to TTC needed 34 so being a bad speedrunner i decided that the one with 6 toads and 16 stars became the main one.
Then came the part of deciding where the run ends i thought it needed to be more different from 16 star so i decided to use the dumbest one.
WDW Clip, the trick that new 16 star runners did many times accidentally became the way to end the run only because it was the dumbest one.
With that idea in my mind i created a google doc file for it but im not sure who did the first run but the first three were these:

1st mrlink2k 24:46.73 (EMU USA)
2nd SioN 35:32.32 (N64 PAL)
3rd DNVIC 36:01.18 (EMU USA)

According to google docs these were added 18 Nov 2017 but sadly no video has survived of these
