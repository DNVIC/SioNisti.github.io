RULES, MAPS, AND ROUTES

RULES FOR On the Moons:

The goal is to get the heart piece from the guy on the roof in kakariko. The timing starts on file select. For emulator use Project64 or OpenEMU. Time ends when you see link hold the heart piece

RULES Salute%:

The goal is to make child link "salute" done by taking out your sword and then using the hookshot. The timing starts on file select. For emulator use Project64 or OpenEMU. Time ends when you see links hand out and not moving

RULES TIWOTR:

The goal is to throw all the chicken is kakariko to the roof above the normal place where you throw them and then getting the bottle from anju. The timing starts on file select. For emulator use Project64 or OpenEMU. Time ends when you see link hold the bottle
