Rules

Timing begins when you enter the Dungeon loading zone for the first time. Timing ends when you enter the blue warp after defeating the Dungeon Boss (Or at obtaining Iron Boots/Ice Arrows/slaying Ganon).

This must be the first time you enter the Deku Tree as denoted by the cutscene that plays when you enter a dungeon for the first time; however you may collect any item, be any form (Adult or Child Link), and collect any upgrade before entering the Deku Tree for the first time.
